Introduction to Da nang

  1. Country name : Vietnam
  2. City name : Da Nang
  3. Population : About 1.22 million
  4. Major features
    • Da Nang is a famous tourist destination in Vietnam.
    • To the east of the city, there is a long beach called My Khe Beach.
    • The Han River, which has the same name as the Han River in Seoul, South Korea, also exists in Da Nang.
    • Da Nang has a dry season from January to August with little rain, and a rainy season from September to December with a lot of rain.
