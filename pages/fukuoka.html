Introduction to Fukuoka

  1. Country name : Japan
  2. City name : Fukuoka
  3. Population : About 1.629 million
  4. Major features
    • Fukuoka is the closest Japanese city to Korea.
    • The airport is near the city, so accessibility is good.
    • For me, Fukuoka food was the most delicious in Japan.
    • Compared to Tokyo and Osaka, Fukuoka is quieter and cheaper.
