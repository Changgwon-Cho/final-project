Introduction to Bohol

  1. Country name : Philippines
  2. City name : Bohol(Tagbilaran)
  3. Population : About 1.31 million
  4. Major features
    • Bohol is a province in the Central Visayas region of the Philippines.
    • The Philippines, the country to which Bohol Province belongs, is a country made up of approximately 7,000 islands.
    • Bohol, along with Cebu, is one of the most visited resorts by Koreans.
    • Bohol has a variety of great tours for tourists(for example, hopping tours, land tours, etc.).
