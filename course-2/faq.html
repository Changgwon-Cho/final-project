Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Seoul famous for?

Seoul is famous for its rich history, vibrant nightlife, and delicious food.

What is the best time to visit Seoul?

Spring (March-May) and Autumn (September-November) are the best seasons to visit Seoul.

Is public transport good in Seoul?

Yes, Seoul has a highly efficient metro and bus system.

What kind of travel information can I find on this website?

You can find information about tourist attractions, famous local foods, and map-based travel guides.

What criteria were used to select the tourist attractions and famous foods for each city?

I selected tourist attractions that I personally visited and enjoyed, as well as places that are highly popular among travelers. Similarly, the food selections are based on my personal experiences and widely recognized local favorites.

Can this website be used on any device?

Yes! The website is designed with responsive web design, ensuring a smooth experience on PCs, tablets, and smartphones.

Can I save or share city information?

Currently, the website does not provide a built-in feature for saving or sharing information, but this functionality is planned for future updates.
