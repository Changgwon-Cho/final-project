About My Website

My Travel Diary is a website created to document the memories of my travels with my girlfriend after the COVID-19 pandemic. Many Koreans record their cherished travel moments in various but common ways, such as photos, blogs, or diaries. However, as a web developer, I wanted to personally build a website where I could document our memories one by one. Through this process, I aim to continuously improve my skills by adding new features to the site, making the experience even more meaningful. Thank you for visiting my humble page. I hope you have a great time!

Features of My Website

  • Users can check the basic information about the countries of the cities I have visited on the main page, including the capital, language, currency, time zone, famous cities, best travel seasons, and more.
  • On the main page, when users hover over the box introducing each country, they can see a list of cities I have visited. By clicking on a city name, they will be redirected to the respective city page.
  • On each cityâ€™s introduction page, users can explore famous tourist attractions, local foods, and photos I personally took in a slider format.
  • Users can navigate between pages using the top navigation bar. On the About page, they can find a brief introduction to my website and click on the map icon to view all the cities I have visited at a glance on a map.
  • On the Contact page, users can send me their basic information and navigate to my social media and GitHub.
  • Users can experience a flexible website on any device thanks to the responsive web design applied to the site.
