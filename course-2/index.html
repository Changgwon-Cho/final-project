Introduction to South Korea

  • Capital : Seoul
  • Language : Korean (Hanguel)
  • Currency : South Korean Won (KRW, 원)
  • Time Zone : Korean Standard Time (KST, UTC+9)
  • Famous Cities : Seoul, Busan, Gyeongju, Jeju Island
  • Best Travel Seasons : Spring(March - May), Autumn(September - November)

Introduction to Japan

  • Capital : Tokyo
  • Language : Japanese
  • Currency : Japanese Yen (JPY, ¥)
  • Time Zone : Japan Standard Time (JST, UTC+9)
  • Famous Cities : Tokyo, Kyoto, Osaka, Fukuoka, Hokkaido
  • Best Travel Seasons : Spring(March - May), Autumn(September - November), Winter(December - February)

Introduction to Vietnam

  • Capital : Hanoi
  • Language : Vietnamese
  • Currency : Vietnamese Dong (VND, ₫)
  • Time Zone : Indochina Time (ICT, UTC+7)
  • Famous Cities : Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City, Da Nang, Hoi An, Nha Trang
  • Best Travel Seasons : Spring(March - May), Autumn(September - November)

Introduction to Malaysia

  • Capital : Kuala Lumpur
  • Language : Malay & English widely spoken
  • Currency : Malaysian Ringgit (MYR, RM)
  • Time Zone : Malaysia Time (MYT, UTC+8)
  • Famous Cities : Kuala Lumpur, Kota Kinabalu
  • Best Travel Seasons : Dry Season(December - March, June-September)

Introduction to Philippines

  • Capital : Manila
  • Language : Filipino & English widely spoken
  • Currency : Philippine Peso (PHP, ₱)
  • Time Zone : Philippine Time (PHT, UTC+8)
  • Famous Cities : Manila, Cebu, Boracay, Bohol
  • Best Travel Seasons : Dry Season(December - May)
